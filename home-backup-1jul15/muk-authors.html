Contribute to Comprehensive Bengali free thought movement!

Mukto-Mona  is one of the heavily visited Bangladeshi secular sites, mainly run by a team of dedicated unpaid volunteers.   In fact, this is the first Bengali site that is dedicated to the growth and maintenance of the most comprehensive free thought movement on the Internet by focusing on science, rationalism, secularism and humanism.  Today Mukto-Mona has reached to a firm level that  the progressive writers in many cyber e-forums are frequently attributed as "Mukto-Mona writers". Many of the contemporary writers and supporters who have published their articles for Mukto-Mona are distinguished scholars in their own discipline. If you are a writer or scholar, and agree with our vision we will very much like to publish your work in our site. 

Send your scholarly written article, backed by research and documentation and references for publication in the Articles section of this site. Articles can be of any length.

Here is the address for submission.

mukto-mona@yahoogroups.com  

If you think you might be interested in volunteering your time in some way to help us with our efforts, please e-mail us.
