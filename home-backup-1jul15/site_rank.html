Responding to our Readers' enquiries...
Who's leading in the Market? Mukto-mona, Shodalap or Vinnomot?

... Help the wretched family of the late journalist Manik Shaha ... 

Over the last couple of years we are bombarded  by hundreds of e-mails from our mambers and readers asking: How many people read Mukto-mona website daily? Is readership of MM increasing? Decreasing? How many hits you get monthly? How does Mukto-mona compare to other similar websites? Is vinnomot much popular compared to MM? How much is shodalap's readership? Who is the most popular among the three?
 

"Well...it's a very sensitive and complicated issue", we would have answered - because it really was, at least initially. But, now - by the grace of  alexa.com - we can share the information about how Mukto-mona has been ranked (as of today Jan 26, 2004) compared, for perspective, to a sampling of other websites like vinnomot, shodalap, banglaamar, jamatepislami, hrcbm, drishtipat etc.- some are progressive, some humanitarian, some are theocratic or perhaps even communal. 

Alexa ranks every site on the web based on number of users and number of pageturns over a 3-month period of time to calculate a website's popularity. The result shows that internationally we're "not at the top" yet - but we're gradually moving up. Nevertheless, among the similar type of Bengali e-forums and web-sites, Mukto-mona's readership has been ranked much higher position compared to others.
