Mukto-Mona is Publishing its First Book...

The first book Alo Hate Choliyache A(n)dharer Jatri (Bangla) by Avijit Roy  is scheduled to appear in February 2005 (by Ankur Prokashoni, Bangladesh). Some of its portions has been published in brief in Mukto-Mona and some other online sites and Printed Magazines.

This book is for those who are not scientists but have an interest in Physics, particularly history of cosmology, black holes, Big Bang etc. The book tries to sketch a stormy road traveled since the science revolution of the 17th century and leads us up to the present.

It started around fifteen billion years ago during the Big Bang -that explosion that had blown out all of the matter in the universe. The Big Bang didn't simply occur in the void of space, it created space as well. But what today's brilliant scientists are looking for, partially, is knowledge as to whether the fundamental forces of physics existed or not within the first few billionths of a second after the explosion or whether or not time existed either.

This book is a thorough journey from ancient period to today and will focus a little more about the universe and its creator, if any.
