What is Rationalism
Rationalism is an attitude that accepts the primacy of reason and aims at establishing a system of philosophy and ethics verifiable by experience, independent of all arbitrary assumptions of authority.  Rationalists argue for a rational and scientific approach to human problems, suggest reasoned alternatives to religious dogmas and superstition, defend freedom of thought, civil liberties and strive for the secularization of politics, society and educational system by building an open society.
