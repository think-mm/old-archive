Call for articles for Bangla New year/Pohela Baishakh

Please send your write-ups to mukto-mona@yahoogroups.com  

On behalf of MM Editorial board we would like to ask our members to send articles/poems for the celebration of forthcoming Bangla New year (Pohela Baishakh). Please send articles written in Bangla and English both on different topics related to this day. Some of the suggested topic may include:. for example,

  • * A usual article signifying Bengali New Year / Pohela Baishakh,
  • * How it is celebrated in Bangladesh and India.
  • * How important this celebration is in the making of our Bengali identity - the example of which is the bombing at Ramna some years back,
  • * A humorous/sarcastic/normal writing on the food dishes that are consumed on this day - i.e. the pitha, panta etc.,
  • * The history of this particular celebration and the evolution of the form of celebration over time etc.

These are just some ideas, feel free to send your write ups on any related topic. We are sorry about the short notice though ...

MM Editorial Board

12 April 2005
