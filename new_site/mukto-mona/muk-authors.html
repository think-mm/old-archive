Contribute to Comprehensive Bengali free thought movement!

Mukto-Mona is one of the most frequently visited Bengali and South Asian secular humanist websites, chiefly run by a team of dedicated volunteers. In fact, this is the first Bengali site which is dedicated to the growth and promotion of a comprehensive free thought movement on the Internet by focusing on science, rationalism, secularism and humanism. In recognition of the contribution made by ‘Muktomona’ website in secular, democratic, social and cultural movement in 2007 Mukto-Mona achieved prestigious Jahanara Imam award. Mukto-Mona has reached a steady level that  some of the most well-known e-columnists of South Asian origin today are called "Mukto-Mona writers". Many of the contemporary writers and supporters who have published their articles in Mukto-Mona are distinguished scholars in their own disciplines. If you are a writer/scholar/activist and agree with our visions, we would be very happy to publish your work in our site. 

Please send your thoughtful and well-written articles substantiated by references, acknowledgements wherever applicable, for publication in our articles section. Articles could be of any length.

Here is the address for submission.

mukto-mona@yahoogroups.com  

If you think you might be interested in volunteering your time in some way to help us with our efforts, please e-mail us.
