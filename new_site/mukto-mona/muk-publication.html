Mukto-Mona has Published Bonna Ahmed's "Bibortoner Poth Dhore" in  2007...

Bonna Ahmed's  'Bibortoner Poth Dhore' has come out in Bookstores in Bangladesh and USA.......

The first book of Bonna Ahmed named "Bibortoner Poth Dhore" (Following the path of Evolution)  has been published in February book fair (2007) at Dhaka,  from Abosar Prokashona, Bangladesh.  The theme the book was discussed in a prestigious seminar consisting biologists, anthropologists, physicists, teachers, students and other professional people.  Detailed report is kept  here

This clear, lively, and systematic presentation examines the scientific evidence for evolution and reaches for the widest possible audience—from scientific minds to those with no science background at all. Bonna Ahmed in her book presents vigorous arguments for the theory of evolution by means of natural selection and other evolutionary process. For the first time in Bangla,  a sound critique of Intelligent Design has been included which argues that ID proponents are not only pseudoscientists, but also a part of a larger social conservative agenda. This book is thorough, up-to-date and provides refutations for every creationists' argument or claim that is inaccurately made against evolution.

MM has Published Avijit Roy & Farid Ahmed's "Mohabisshe Pran O Budhimottar Khoje"...

Avijit & Farid's "Mohabisshe Pran O Buddhimottar Khoje" has come out in Bookstores in Bangladesh and USA.......

Abosar has published another important book named "Mohabisshe Pran O Buddhimottar Khoje" (In search of life and intelligence in the universe) by Avijit Roy & Farid Ahmed in February book fair in Dhaka, 2007.  The theme of the book is also discussed in the same seminar.  Detailed report is kept  here

When the earth formed some 4.5 billion years ago, it was a lifeless, inhospitable place. A billion years later it was teeming with organisms resembling blue-green algae. How did they get there? How the first life formed? and most importantly, are we alone in the universe? These question that everyone perhaps has asked at some time—and scientists in particular want an answer. Avijit Roy & Farid Ahmed have written in a very lucid language focusing these issues and the recent advancement in the field. This is an outstanding little book that discloses a myriad of possibilities about the origin of life on earth and beyond.

Ananta Bijoy has Published "Yukti"...

Yukti  has come out in Bookstores in Bangladesh ......

An important little magazine named "Yukti" (logic) edited by MM member Ananta Bijoy Das has been published in February book fair 2007.  See the  detailed report from Bangla newspaper Jugantor

Ananta Bijoy Dash, MM member and founder of  Rationalist Association of Sylhet, published a rare collection that is a house for humanism, freethought, skepticism and rationalism. The essays addressed the fundamental issues of how scientific naturalism, rational thought, and secular humanism, feminism and atheism  contributing to the new global civilization that is emerging today with all its painful adjustments, changes, and challenges. A strongly recommended acquisition for personal, academic, and community library.

Mukto-Mona has Published its First Book...

 'Alo Hate Choliyache A(n)dharer Jatri' has come out in Bookstores in Bangladesh, USA, Singapore.........

The first book Alo Hate Choliyache A(n)dharer Jatri (Bangla) by Avijit Roy  has been published in March 22, 2005 from Ankur Prokashoni, Bangladesh.  Detailed report is kept  here

This book describes a brief and lucid history of the creation of the universe from a scientific point of view. This book is for those who are not scientists but have an interest in physics, particularly history of cosmology, black holes, Big Bang etc. The book tries to sketch a stormy road traveled since the science revolution of the 17th century and leads us up to the present and offers a naturalistic explanation for empirical observations that are frequently given theistic interpretations.
