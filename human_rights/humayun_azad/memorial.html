Humayun Azad Foundation Formed (click here)

Latest: An evening with the family of late Dr. Humayun Azad. by Dr. Ajoy Roy 16/01/2005

On MM member's request Mrs. Azad (Latifa Khanam), wife of  late Dr. Humayun  Azad - our beloved Mukto-Mona member,  and their elder daughter Mouli agreed to accept a sum as a donation from MM to the ‘Azad Memorial Fund’, which the family would be raising to keep alive the name of Dr. Humayun Azad, e.g. setting up a library in his village home, a desire of late Humaun Azad and a scholarship in his name for meritorious and poor students. These are just some proposals with many others came up during the discourse. 

This is, however, entirely a family project. Mukto-Mona (MM) will  no way consider it as its project, long or short term. If some members of MM, and well-wishers want to donate a sum to ‘Azad Memorial Fund’ thru MM, they should do by the following link. MM would only act as a carrier of the fund to the family. Our Prime Target is 2000 Sing$ for time being.
