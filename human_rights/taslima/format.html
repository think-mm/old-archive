Stop Muzzling Taslima Nasrin a la Bangladeshi style

 

What we can do now?

Mukto-mona  encourages to build international public opinion to appeal to the judiciary of Bangladesh to annul the recent verdict against Ms. Taslima Nasrin.  You are requested to do everything possible to support the efforts of Mukto-mona in this issue.

We request you to write letters appealing to the Bangladesh Government ( secretary@pmo.bdonline.com, ssa@pmobd.org ) in the format kept in our web-site or in similar lines with copy to Mukto-mona Moderator [ mukto-mona-owner@yahoogroups.com, mukto_mona2000@yahoo.com ] .

 

Format of the letter:

      

 

To: Bangladesh Government ( secretary@pmo.bdonline.com, ssa@pmobd.org )

Copy to: Mukto-mona Moderator [ mukto-mona-owner@yahoogroups.com, mukto_mona2000@yahoo.com ]

Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh

Your Excellency,

I am shocked to hear that humanist writer Dr. Taslima Nasrin, currently in exile being accused of "hurting religious sentiments of followers of Islam" through her writings and  convicted to one year imprisonment by the verdict of a magisterial court of Gopalganj district of your country.

I feel It is the right of everyone to express freely one's opinion in any civilized society and I hope that you will guide Bangladesh to an open and pluralist society.

I appeal through you to the judiciary of Bangladesh to annul the verdict against Ms. Taslima Nasrin since she was not provided by the court with adequate defence facilities or a defence councillor - to which she was legally entitled. I strongly support the international campaign in defence of Dr. Nasrin. 

(Signature)

(Name and address)

 
  • If you have a web page or site, please post this message with a link to www.mukto-mona.com. 

  • If you have a journal or if you have access to the media, please try to bring out stories about Dr. Nasrin.  Remember: strong public opinion is necessary for the success of any mediation or diplomatic initiatives.
