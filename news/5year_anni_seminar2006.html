5th Year of Reason, Science & Understanding:
A Seminar on secularism on the Occasion of
Mukto-mona’s 5th Anniversary!


 

Shiksa Andolan Mancha and Mukto-mona are jointly holding a daylong seminar on Friday, August 11, 2006 at the R C Majumdar Conference Hall, faculty of arts, Arts Building, DU campus. The theme of the seminar is “Secularism, Society and State: Perspective Bangladesh”. On behalf of the two organizations we cordially invite you to participate effectively in the daylong seminar. See detail program below.
