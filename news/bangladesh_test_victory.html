Tigers achieved first Test triumph

Bangladesh achieves her maiden Test win when they beat Zimbabwe by a massive 226 runs on the 5th day of the first Test at Chittagong. Enamul Huq Moni ended with 6 for 45 as Zimbabwe folded for 154 a little after lunch on the final day.
