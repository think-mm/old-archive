December 25, 2007

Invitation to Join & Help Bangladesh Genocide Study & Research Group at Kean University, NJ!

 


Bangladesh Genocide: 1971
[Picture source: Liberation War Museum]

Dear All,
This is a formal invitation on behalf of Kean University and Bangladesh Genocide Study Group (BGSG) to all of you to join the BGSG (email: bdgenostudy@gmail.com) and help us to create a research network and gather resources on Bangladesh Genocide. All the resources will be used to develop the ground work toward writing a course curriculum on Bangladesh Genocide at Kean University .

You are cordially invited to join the BGSG with your research work, resources and your network with other researchers and evidence collectors. BGSG and the University administration will honor your work and will greatly appreciate your contribution. BGSG and the university will work together to house the materials collected through the network in one place in the Holocaust Resource Center.

BGSG and University administration will also appreciate if you can pass this invitation to more Bangladesh researchers and evidence collectors in Bangladesh as well as other countries.

We appreciate your support and cooperation.

Best wishes for the holidays.
