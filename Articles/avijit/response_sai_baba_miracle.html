Ok, I am trying to put some rational thoughts on so-called "miracle" of ash formation from Sai-baba's picture etc. There are three kinds of explanation available in the market. Firstly, it is entirely possible that some followers are faking it (i.e spreading the story) to make themselves and their Saibaba "great" to the public (this glorification occasionally happens for the celebrities and religious gurus). Second explanation is - the top of the photo frame is made intentionally hollow and filled with vibhuti or ash, so vibhuti falls down time to time from the framed photographs of Satya Sai Baba.  But third explanation is most logical. It says that Sai baba or their followers  sprays lactic acid (crystal) on the glass frame of the picture prior to marketing. Lactic acid forms ash-like particles from the glass being exposed to air. If common people are not aware of  the mystery (just chemical reaction) behind ash formation, they may get astonished.
