I need to write about my great sense of shame about my country and the course it is taking.  As a Euro-American of several generations, I have no way to separate myself from the horrors my country is committing.

I want to blame it on our appointed President who thinks there is a god who told him to do this, but the truth is that he couldn't get by with it if we were not such a bellicose people.

We have no excuse.  We are wealthy, reasonably well-educated, certainly well treated by the world.  Of course, a large minority of us are outraged, will have no part of this, but we cannot stop the war-hunger of our compatriots.

I am so ashamed.
