Popular doctor S.K. Mukherjee stabbed to death

Date line: Jhenaidah, Thursday 18th September, 2003: All daily news papers make a leading news of killing a veteran doctor Mr. S K Mukherjee, a very well known personality of Jhenaidah. Who could imagine that silently and secretly he had become a target of militant groups. After Khulna, the militants, whose identity has not been yet neither known nor any terrorist group claimed the credit of killing of the octogerian popular physician and local socio-political leader of the locality, once again committed a major crime in broad daylight.  

Doctor Mukherjee was a founder president of Khulna branch of BMCA (Bangladeh Medical Association), now a sharply divided between pro-government and anti-government groups. He was also an influential local leader (erstwhile vice president) of Awami League, erstwhile president of Bangabandhu Parisad and erstwhile vice president of Hindu Buddha Christian Oikya Parisad. He was the president of district Puja Observance Committee. He was a staunch believer of liberal democracy and secularism. Although he was leading a retired life from active politics keeping himself busy only with his medical profession and social works, his enemies perhaps did not forget his past. As usual police is yet to find motive of the murder and not to speak of finding or arresting his killers. Of course no body has any confidence in our police system.   

As has been revealed on Thursday 17th September he has been busy supervising renovation work of his residence. He was upstairs, when the doorbell rang. He came down to open the door through his chamber at downstairs. This was around 2 p.m. As no response was heard for half an hour, the curious maid servant (Anna) went for looking her master. To her surprise, the chamber was found locked from inside. So she peeped through the window to find horrifying scene that ‘daktar babu’ was lying in a pool of blood. It was known later that he was stabbed at several parts of his body by sharp knives by armed groups of 5-6 men. The neighbors, as soon as they heard the screaming sound of Anna, rushed to the place of occurrence. Doctor was taken to Jhenaidah General Hospital where he was declared dead by the attending doctors. This incident brought an atmosphere of shock and horror in the town and condemned by every peace loving citizens of the town. According to some neighbors the doctor was from some time being harassed and threatened for extortion by a group of armed miscreants.  

During the incident AL chief was addressing a public meeting at Jhenaidah. On hearing the incidence she rushed to the residence of doctor Mukherjee and gave solace to the members of bereaved family. She accused that the terrorists and militants under the shelter of the 4-party alliance government led by Khaleda Begum must bear the responsibility of the crime committed. She demanded immediate arrest of the killers and bring them to trial. Is the demand of Sheikh Hasina is just a political rhetoric or do have some grain of truth. Our most able home minister must provide the answer instead of saying “Yakahn yar samoy hobe takhan take chole yete hobe- this is the desire of God. Is isn’t it ?” (When time comes he would have to leave- this is the decree of almighty Allah).  He may further add, “Mukherjee babu nirvan lav korechhen.” Who cleared the path of Nirvan – this is immaterial.        

Our only anxiety is who is the next target after Muhuri, Jnanjyoti, Manikchhari priest, Shrikant Rakshit, and host of other victims.... ?
