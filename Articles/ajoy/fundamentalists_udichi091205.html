What happened at Netrokona ?

 

Netrokona is a small district, once under the greater Mymensigh. Early in the morning 9-30 a.m. to be exact, the enthusiastic workers of Netrokona branch Udichi started assembling near the place where Udichi’s annual function were to start at 10 a.m. Many of their central committee members were also expected to come from Dhaka. It was learnt that a small packet was found laying the place of the dais. When attention was drawn to the attending security personnel, they identified to be an explosive device, and took immediate steps to detonate it. As reported hearing the sound of the blast in the process, a young man wrapped with some wires etc came forward.  The human bomb blasted killing at least seven people and as many as 39 injured with bomb splinters.

 

The killed and wounded persons were taken to Netrokona Hospital and private hospitals & clinics. Later on serious ones were transferred to Mymensigh district hospital and Medical College Hospital. Whether Yadab is really the human bomb or he is like many an onlookers remains to be seen- and the real human bomb or bomb thrower successfully had camouflaged the entire incident. A cleaver and well thought out plan indeed.

Protests in Dhaka city   

 

From the students, at DU campus the recently formed Sangarm Parisad of seven students’ organizations brought out processions and held rallies blaming the 4-part alliance government’s for Netrokona killing of Udichi workers and innocent people. The rally demanded resignation of the government. On the other hand leaders of the BNP student front blamed AL for all recent bomb killings including the incident of Jhalkathi, Gazipur and now of Netrokona. We do not blame the BNP student front because they have no independent existence- so they have to echo their chairperson’s voice full throated.

 

All political parties opposition to government brought processions, protest rallies, held meetings condemning government’s utter failure to protect lives of innocent people. Dhaka Udichi brought out a big procession condemning atrocious and repeated attack upon on them. They clearly blamed the Police and RAB for manufacturing ‘Yadab story’ in an attempt to shift the blame from the militant Islamic fundamentalist to a communal channel. The leaders opined expressively that all bomb attacks have been and are being carried out by Jamat and other Islamic parties with a view to establish a Islamic system in Bangladesh.

 

The AL general secretary and other AL leaders in a meeting held in front of their party office at Bangabandhu Avenue, blamed the government for bomb attacks on judges, officials, secular peoples and cultural organizations are the work of Islamic fundamentalists sitting with the government. The Yadav story, a creation of the police at the instance of Jamatis, is to camouflage the real issue. The meeting claimed the government failure and demanded resignation of the government.
