Shabdaguchha Poetry Festival

 

Dear Friends:
The Second Shabdaguchha Poetry Festival will be held on 14th April, 2007 in Astoria, Queens. (Address: 23-63 Steinway Street. Time: 3pm to 10pm). Through the events we will also celebrate the Bengali New Year, 1414.
