Jamal & Others

Shahanur Islam

Name of the perpetrators                                : Joint Forces, Dhaka Cantonment, Dhaka.

Date & Time of Incident                                  : 22 June, around 2.30 am to 3 pm

Place where the victim (died) was caught : Tea stalls at the Bottala of Mirpur-14.

Place of Incident                                             : Dhaka Cantonment, Dhaka. 

Fact in Brief:

On 22 June 2007, around 2.30am to 3am the members of the joint force’s arrested Md. Jalal Uddin  and other umbers-around 10 to12 innocent people including  Rabiul, Mona, Kajol, and Shah Alam, who were the driver and helper of motor vehicle locally known as human holler, and took them in to the Mirpur Cantonment from Mirpur-14, where  Md. Jalal uddin  was working at tea stall and others were sleeping in to their motor vehicle at that time, suspecting them as a thieves who were committed theft at the house of sergeant Md. Sirajul Islam, who is in Army officer, breaking lock of his home at that night. After taking them in Cantonment the members of joint forces tortured them brutally from 3am of 21st June to 1pm of 22. June 2007. Members of joint force’s tied all the victims separately and bit them in several ways. On 22 June of 2007 was Friday so the members of joint forces gave a break of two hours (1pm to 3pm) for prayer in torturing the victims. In the mean time the physical condition of Kajol and Jamal, who were the youngest among the victims had become worst and realizing that the members of joint force’s handed over the victim in to the Kafrul Police station and the sergeant of Army Md. Sirajul Islam logged an F.I.R. that number-380511 dated 22 June 2007 in the said police station. Thereafter, the authority of Kafrul police Station admitted victim Jamal and Kajoll at the Dhaka Medical Collage Hospital and send others to Jail Custody. On 22nd June, at 3.30am Jamal died due to inhuman torture. His body bore a number of injury marks in various parts and Kajol was fighting for his life, beside this other victims is going to being physically disabled for life time.

 

Statement of Shah Alam:

 Sah Alam who was tortured brutally by the members of Joint Forces and has also saw the torture scene of Jamal who was died in such instance, said to the Fact finding team of BIHR “On 21th June 2007, around 2.30am to 3am the members of the joint force’s arrest me and another 10/12 person including Jamal from the tea stall which is situated at human holler stand at Mirpur-14 and bring them in Cantonment, and then placed us in to a dark room. Later on the Joint Forces tie us with tee and starts to hit us brutally by one after another. After watching the torture of others Jamal says me that I will die if they hit me like this and then Jamal touch the foot of those perpetrators and Jamal also call them Father. The level of their torture was so dangerous that I thought that I will not alive. Jamal was a good boy and also had good moral character and his tea stall was situated at the turn nearer to the bus stand and that is why it was a very tea stall. And Jamal earns considerable money from such tea stall. i was shocked when I heard the news of death of Jamal from a constable of Kafrul  police Station. After then one question arise in my mind that is what will happen to this country.

 

Statement of Rabiul Islam:

Rabiul Islam who is a driver of human holler, also tortured by the members of joint forces and watched the scene of torture of Jamal said to the fact finding team of BIHR “On 21st June 2007, around 2.30am to 3am the members of the joint force’s arrest me and other victims from the human holler stand and bring us to the cantonment and tie us with tree and start to beat us after hanging in the suspecting us as thieves. We fall in sick after due to torture but Jamal and Kajol fall in seriously sick. We can not take easily the death of Jamal. Every person who lives in this area has cried hearing the death news of Jamal.”

 

Rabiul Islam also said to the fact finding team of BIHR “I can not work properly after being tortured in such away by the members of Joint Forces .My hands and legs are still paining. On the other hand there is a fear in my mind about the possibility of occurring the incident of torture with me by the members of the Joint Forces again because we are wrongdoers because we are poor people.”

 

 Statement of Md. Mona:

Md. Mona is a driver of human holler who was tortured by the members of joint forces and also watched the scene of torture of Jamal said to the fact finding team of BIHR “On 21st June 2007, around 2.30am to 3am the members of the joint force’s arrest me along with other victims from the human holler stand and bring us to the cantonment and tie us with tree and start to beat us hanging with tree. Members of the joint forces beat Jamal using various methods for compel to give confession regarding to committing such steal.” Mona also said “none of us thought that we would able to alive again. Though I am alive by the kindness of Allah, but the memory of such torture comes back again and again and I can understand what our fault was and what the reason behind Jamal’s death is”.

 

Statement of Jamal’s Uncle:

Ali Ajgor who is a uncle of Jamal said to the fact finding team of BIHR “Jamal has been working in my shop for last three years. My shop was opened for day 24 hours. I was in the charge of my shop at morning and Jamal was in the charge of such shop at evening.  On 21st of June I give the charge of my shop to Jamal at night.  Next morning the security guard who was in the duty of the area where my shop is situated comes in my house and tell me about the incident of arrest of Jamal along with other victims by the members of Joint Forces .After hearing all the incident I went to cantonment and tell the total theme of the incident to the Army officer who was in the duty at gate and I also request them to escape Jamal. But after hearing me the concerned Army officer do misbehave with me and also tell me to get lost from there.  Then I leave that with a view in my mind that the Army Officers will not cause any damage to Jamal and will leave Jamal after some interrogation. Jamal’s father came to Dhaka in order to bring his son with him to village. Though he was able to bring his to village but not alive .I can not understand why the members of Joint Forces killed an innocent boy like Jamal.”

 

Statement of Akther Hossain:

Akther Hossain who is the owner of the house where Jamal was tenure said to the fact finding team of BIHR “Very people in this world are as good as jamal. No one in this area could not tell that Jamal has done misbehave with him. His world was limited in shop to home. Besides this a little boy like him can not take part in any such kind of offence. How the Joint Forces could killed him without any investigation and proof of the fact. I can’t understand why all these happened”

 

Statement of Osman Mollah:

Osman Mollah who has a fruit shop in front of Jamal’s shop said to the fact finding team of BIHR “Jamal was a good boy.  He was jolly all the time. I have never seen him to do any wrong behave with others. I was with him for a long time so that I can’t understand why this happens to him. Lots of offender is walking around openly but they are not arrested yet while innocent boy like Jamal died due to torture by the members of Joint forces. So there is no justice in our country”

 

He also said“we are aged person so that why don’t they kill us; at least young innocent boy like him can alive in this way”

 

Statement of Monir Hossain:

Human holler driver Monir Hossain said to the fact finding team of BIHR “I know Jamal for long period and I am a customer of his shop. I have never seen him to do any wrong behave with others. All the people of bottala feel sorrow hearing the news of Jamal’s death. If the incident took place in any other time than the time of emergency more than thousands of people would show the demonstration protesting such kind of brutality.”

 

Monir also said “What kind of rule is this that an innocent boy killed without committing any offence, should we take it in a easy way”

 

Statement of DR.M A Kashem:

Dr. MA Kashem who was treated Jamal at Dhaka Medical College Hospital said to the fact –Finding team that Jamal died due to inhuman torture. His body bore a number of injury marks in various parts and bruises were found on the chest.

 

Statement of Investigation Officer:

Md. Alamgir Hossain who is the investigation officer of the case that was filed by the Sergeant Sirajul Islam against the victims said to the fact finding team, “ I was out of the police station during the time of handed over the victim to the police station for my as usual duty. I came back to the police station after receiving a call from the police station and see the victim Jamal and Kajol as sick. There after i admitted them in Dhaka Medical College Hospital”. He again said, “The investigation regarding to disclose the cause of Jamal death is ongoing. So, we can brief nothing before completing Investigation”.

 

Case against the Victims:

On 22 June 2007 around 3.30 am two thieves named Al Amin and Saddam Hossain were caught by the on duty military police during the time of entering the house of Khurshid who is an army officer that house number of 566/9 of Rajanigondha officers Mess in Dhaka breaking the door lock for committing steal. During the interrogation they mentioned their some other accomplices. According to their confession the army men conducted an operation beside the area and arrested the above mentioned innocent bus driver and helper and took them the army camp and tortured them brutally. Thereafter at 3.10 pm they were handed over the Kafrul Thana filing a case that no. 59 of 2007 under section of 380/580 of penal code (suspected as theft). There after on 23 June 2007 they (except Jamal) were forwarded to the CMM court and the learned court sent them in jail Custody. There after on 03 and 04 July 2007 they were released from the jail after getting bail from the court.

 

Observation of BIHR

Report published on newspaper, statement of neighbors and co-victims observation of BIHR is that Jamal and others are arrested and victimized in brutally torture due to suspicion as involving committing theft through breaking lock of the door of the house of Khurshid who is an army officer that house number of 566/9 of Rajanigondha officers Mess in Dhaka, which is violation of fundamental rights as well as human rights. Here specially mention that the perpetuators were not only torture them but also implicated with false case and send them Jail Custody. 

Action requested: 

1.                  An independent commission headed by a High Court Judge is to be instituted with a view to enquire into the matter to ascertain the cause of death of Jamal and torture of others;

2.                  publish the report at the earliest;

3.                  ensure exemplary punishment of the perpetrators who were directly and indirectly involved in this gross violation of human rights;

4.                  provide adequate financial compensation to the victims and victim’s family;

5.                  Guarantee the physical and psychological integrity of victims and victims family

6.                  Ensure the respect of human rights and fundamental freedoms throughout the country in accordance with national laws and international human rights standards.

7.                  provide human rights  education and  training to the Police Inspectors and policemen who run police stations;

8.                  Bar the police not to misuse Section 54 of Cr. P.C to harass the innocent people.  It is to be here in this regard that such incidences have increased to an alarming proportion.

9.                  Remove reservation from Art.14 of Convention against Torture.  

N:B: This Fact–Finding has been conducted and Report has been prepared by the three members fact-finding team of Bangladesh Institute of Human Rights (BIHR) in cooperation with the Centre for Fact-Finding and Research (CHFR) comprising Rafikul Islam, Adv.
