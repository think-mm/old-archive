THE THIRD REASON OF

BLINDNESS

  Bijon B. Sarma

  

STORY OF CUPID :

Cupid, the renowned Greek god was blind. Was he really ? Or, the learned artists/writers ‘pictured’ him as blind, such that they could prove, ‘love is blind’. Let the artists say whatever they like. Take a scalpel and ask them a question “Honorable Sirs, would you mind if I make you blind?” Probably you won’t hear the answer, because by that time they have ran away to be out of your reach. They know “The worst curse of this world is to be blind”.

 

FIRST REASON OF BLINDNESS :

The twentieth century Cupid was taken to a doctor. The doctor checked him. In reply to Cupid’s parents’ questions the doctor said, the reason behind someone’s blindness is physiological, which may happen in the eyes, in the part of the brain controlling the activities of the eyes and/or in the connecting nerves. They could find no such defect in Cupid.

 

CUPID COMPLAINS OF BLINDNESS :

The following week Cupid complained, he does not see. The doctor checked him again and discovered no defect. His parents became anxious with him. At about ten in the night Cupid shouted “Father, mother, please come and see, I am blind again. I cannot see”.

The parents rushed in to discover, everything was all right.

Mother asked “What happened to you, dear ?”

Cupid –       Do you remember mother, the doctor said, there is one reason of blindness ?

Mother –     Yes, we do.

Cupid –     He was wrong. There are two reasons for blindness. The doctor could discover only the first one. I have discovered the second. Some times I become blind due to this reason.

Father –      What is this reason, dear ?   

Cupid had the electric switch in his hand. He turned the switch off. Instantly there was darkness all around. Neither the parents nor Cupid could see anything.

Cupid clapped and laughed loudly.

Then he said – “You see, you are blind now. So, darkness, or want of light is the second cause of blindness. I have discovered the second cause of blindness”. 

 

The parents appreciated his ‘discovery’ and released a great sigh of relief. At last they came to know cause of Cupid’s occasional blindness. WHAT THEY DID NOT KNOW WAS, MILLIONS OF CUPIDS WOULD BECOME BLIND AGAIN and THIS TIME, DARKNESS WOULD NOT BE THE REASON. And this time the recovery of their blindness won’t be in a single electrical switch.  

 

WHAT HAPPENED TO RAM ?

Ram is the only son of his parents. In 1950 AD he lived in a remote village in a poor developing country. His maternal uncle came to the village to see his relatives. While returning, he took little Ram with him. They reached a large town after a two-day long ‘cart-boat-bus-train’ service. One week after Ram was gone his got an information saying, Ram was not all right. Some one gave the information to one of their distant relatives in the neighboring village. Ram’s parents were worried. At his wife’s insistence Ram’s father walked to the nearest post office, which was at a distance of ten kilometers. He arranged to send a letter to his relative in the city. The letter was posted with care. The postmaster assured, he would try that the letter reaches the addressee. Then he told, there was no hope that it would reach within a week. He suggested for sending a telegram.

 

Back at home, Ram’s mother insisted on sending a telegram. This time the old man had to spend a whole day to travel to the nearest railway station. The telegram was sent. The man in charge said, it would reach their distant office within seconds. But it may take some time for their man to deliver to the recipient. And then, it depends upon the recipient when he would send the reply. The man reported it might need four or five days. 

 

Days rolled on. There was no response from any corner. Ram’s mother fell ill. At this stage Ram’s uncle said “Don’t worry, I am going today. You would get the information very soon”.

The man returned after five days. He informed, ‘yes, Ram fell ill. Then he was admitted in a hospital. Now he has recovered and staying in his maternal uncle’s house. He would take rest for another one week as per doctor’s advise’.

At last Ram’s parents released a sigh of great relief. They did not know, after half century, there would be wireless, land-phone, mobile phone at every hand, such that no parents would have to wait with anxious hearts for days together.         

 

WHAT HAPPENED TO RAHUL ?

Rahul is Ram’s grandson. He is a first year student of computer technology and is staying in a distant city. Ram got a message in his mobile phone, it told something about Ram, but he could decipher the meaning. Ram sent an inquiry. He could not reach Rahul’s mobile. Ram’s wife rang the telephone of Rahul’s landlord. The maidservant received the phone and said she would report the matter to her master. Ram’s son contacted Rahul’s university. They replied, they would look into the matter. Rahul’s elder brother sent messages to his friends and relatives in that city. He received as many as sixteen messages. Five told, they would look for Ram and inform later. Three told ‘they were aware Ram was in trouble’. One said ‘Ram was in a type of trouble he cannot disclose’. And four others said, so far they could apprehend Ram was all right. One said, ‘Ram would be coming soon’. From all the sixteen news, all of which traveled within seconds, Rahul’s relatives could not arrive at any decision. It was no wonder that Rahul’s mother was in the bed.        

 

Probably Rahul’s mother is in a better position. The sixteen messages were different because, probably some of them did not know anything about Ram, but did not want to expose that. May be some of them knew the right thing and did not want to disclose because it was not ‘positive’. However, none of these sixteen respondents had financial or any other type of interest associated with Ram’s whereabouts. In case they had, what do you think, would have been the nature of the information Rahul’s relatives would get ?  

 

WHAT HAPPENS WHEN THE RESPONDENTS ARE AGENTS WITH VESTED INTERESTS ?  

Dear readers, can you imagine the situation, when the sixteen respondents are countries or large corporate bodies and their financial or other type of interests are associated with the manipulation of the information ?  What would happen in such a situation ? Won’t Ram really turn to ‘Ram’ (husband of ewe) with numerous radio, television, newspapers, walkie-talkie, wireless sets, land telephone and mobile sets around him ? These items would ‘tremendously help the large manufacturers to earn big profit’ and at the same time, ‘would miserably fail to give Ram the correct information’. Cupid discovered the second cause of blindness, he did not know the third one. 

 

LIGHTS ON CUPID’S EYES :

Those having the experience of interrogation in the torture cell know, the torturers throw a bright light on the face of the man to be tortured. And instantly the man discovers, “too much light is another cause of blindness”. Today’s Cupids are not in the torture chamber.  they know  themselves to have been free citizens of democratic countries. (N.B.  : We discard the case of the Islamic countries. Their citizens are already ‘blind’ due to religion. Absence or over presence of light does not matter anything for the blind people). The Cupids of the developed countries cannot think of the corporate bodies, specially the medias as torturers. That is exactly what happens to men when they turn blind.

They do not know that the medias blind the voters in election, the medias give wrong impression about what happens in various countries, about what happens in the warring countries, about who are responsible for the worsening situations in different regions and so on so forth. 

 

CONCLUSION :

One needs “eyes” to know the reason of blindness. When someone is already blind due to the first, second or the third reason, he loses the scope to know the reason. In the developed countries, the strong corporate bodies and their stronger medias have already blinded the common people. The people of the religious countries, by all means, are blind. Those who, still do not have such strong medias can see, how the medias play notorious roles in deceiving the common people. They witness, how the “medias make the president of USA” and the blindfolded people believe, “they have accomplished the great task”.
