STORIES  OF THE  SPARKING

METEORS

Bijon B. Sarma

 

INTRODUCTION :

Nobel laureate poet Rabindranath Tagore looked at the sky. His name meant the Sun (Rabi). But he could not see the sun, because it was night. In the dark sky the poet, however, saw a sparking meteor to appear, jump and vanish. And he wrote *:

“By fluttering the wings, the meteor

Was on its transitory flight,

It sparked, it flew and got lost,

That was its great delight.”

 

(*The English translation is by me. Please pardon my inability to translate the noble laureate’s poem).

 

The poet saw the sparking meteor to vanish, probably because it really vanished or because it fell on distant land where the poet’s vision could not reach. Not all the meteors vanish in the sky. Some can manage to reach the earth. If those are of minute size one may not be able to detect those. But the remains of some meteors are quite large and capable of bringing profuse changes. Along with some political discussions we shall tell the story of two such meteors in this article.

 

METEOR – 01, BLESSING FROM THE SKY :

The fifty-acre land shows a man-made solar-system, of course in a mini-scale. There are sky-museum, planetarium and other attractive items. Millions of people from home and abroad visit the place every day. The place has acquired a position in the list of the prestigious profit earning organizations of the country. The principal attraction of the place however, is at the center. A five-ton black stone, the remains of a meteor has been preserved with care and caution. For its excellent contribution to national economy, quite often the administrators mention it as a ‘blessing from the blue sky’.  

 

METEOR – 02, CURSE FROM THE HELL :

Immediately after that ‘big fall’, the inhabitants of the land, measuring about fifty square kilometers had to leave the place.  The scientists opined, the remains of the meteor contained radio-active materials. No one ever goes to that place. But still some inhabitants of the neighboring villages at times suffer from mysterious diseases. After careful examination the doctors opine “... originated due to exposure to radioactive materials”. No wonder that the people mention it as ‘The Great Curse’.

 

POETIC FACES OF THE METEORS :

The poets have eyes, differing from those of the common people. The biologist/botanist know flower as the genital mechanism of plants. For the poets, however, flowers are gifts from the heaven. In case our Nobel laureate poet could see the two meteors in two different lands today, he would have seen those differently. Be they curse or blessing they belong to one common class, the meteor. Our poet probably could see them as “Socialism”, because socialism also played such dual roles. Like these meteors, socialism also vanished, but not without remains. Various countries still enjoy or suffer from its remains in various magnitudes.  The poet opined, “It sparked, it flew and got lost, that was its great delight.” In case of Socialism the poet’s observation could have been “It appeared, it burnt houses and fried meats, and then it disappeared”. Those whose house were burnt still curse it with filthy words. Strangely, those whose ‘meat’ it fried, do relish the roasted meat. But do they really know, why and how they were rewarded with these benefits ?

 

THE CONCEPT OF CAPITALIZATION :

Capitalization is an old concept. From time immemorial the experts advised for ‘capitalization of a single item’ as the surest key to success. Suppose a man is taking a program to achieve top level successes in the fields A, B, C and D, second level successes in E, F, G and H and so on. When the resources are limited and situations are not in full control, it is quite doubtful if the man would at all success in his program. But if he stresses upon one and only one item, say A, there is wide possibility that he would come out successful. This is capitalization. In the above program, say  the man is after capitalization in only one field ‘A’. It is obvious that he would succeed, of course at the cost of other fields, viz. B, C, D, E, F, G etc. ‘Specialist professionals’ have capitalized on only one item, where as the ‘generalists’ have done the same on many.           

 

CAPITALIZATION OF MAJORITY’S WILL : DEMOCRACY :

When people wanted to see the reflection of their will in administration at the cost of other related items they devised the system of electing representative by the endorsement of the majority through a process called ‘election’. So democracy is the capitalization of the majority’s will, at the cost of other items including disregard for the will of the minority.  

 

 

CAPITALIZATION OF PEOPLE’S WELFARE : SOCIALISM :

Socialism is the theoretical model for capitalization of welfare of all people, as member of this world and irrespective of their participation or capability in productive jobs. However, at the time of preparing this model the advocators endeavored to take care of some crisis often faced by democracy, or more precisely, the election system of democracy. However, in view of the disappearing scene of Socialism at such an early stage indicates, the theoretical model not sound to withstand the pangs of time.   

 

SOCIALISM TURNS TO COMMUNISM :

Karl Max, Frederick Angels and numerous other persons who worked for ‘Socialism’ might have gotten the inspiration in the following sequences :

 

(i) NOBLE INTENTION OF FAIR DISTRIBUTION  : They thought, the poor and weak people are our brothers. They deserve equal share in the wealth of the world. But they do not get it, because the strong people grab most. We must do something for them. It will be a ‘system of fair distribution for all’. It will be ‘socialism’.  

 

(ii) REFORMATION OF DEMOCRACY : They thought, ‘in the democratic administration the poor and weak have been honored by giving equal right and weight in “Vote”. But the rich do not allow them to apply the same. How can we ensure their right in democracy ?’ They thought and thought over the ‘reason for its manipulation and discovered, ‘the rich can purchase the votes of the poor because they have in their hands items like production houses and growth fields. And they use these for exploiting workers under them’.  Solution ? ‘Such exploitable items would be managed by the state’. The ‘state’ however was later modified by ‘cooperative organizations’.

 

(iii) HOW TO EXECUTE : The next question to be answered was ‘How to execute this concept ?’ They were aware, the rich won’t like to lose their interest. So, they thought of motivating the people whose interest this system would serve. These people are the laborers of production houses and agricultural growth-farms. They thought of ‘strong communes’ of the working people. They also thought of the process in which the working people could form communes and the communes could grab state power. Thus, where ‘socialism’ remained as the objective, they planned for the ‘rule of the communes’. For the time being their  process of achieving the target turned to ‘Communism’.

 

(iv) THE COMMUNIST PARTY : They had to think of the political party, named the “Communist Party”. The members of the commune were to elect their representatives at various levels. On top of all was a chairman. Once elected, he would in fact held ‘dictatorial power’ for certain period of time. (In the presidential form of democracy in the US, the president also have similar power). They observed, if such a party can grab the power, they would run the bureaucracy with the help of the representatives of the communes. They observed, it sounded like ‘dictatorship’. They honestly named it ‘dictatorship of the proletariat’, where proletariat means ‘the working people’. They targeted for improving democracy and now they ended up with a dictatorial system. However, they had the consolation in minds that they were after the noble goal, “Socialism”. Today we know the goal was not achieved and there is little possibility that it would at all be achieved.   

 

PRE-MATURED BIRTH OF COMMUNISM :

The advocators of ‘Socialism through Communism’ presumed an extremely long, tiresome and uneven road on way to achieve their goal. But then they were amazed to see, Communism was born in some countries of Asia and Europe much ahead of their prediction. Those who fathered this Communism, however, did not have any sympathy for communism. In course of their invading program to liberate countries from the grip of some tyrant dictators, a few ‘generals’ all on a sudden discovered, vast land was under their authority. They could not think of following the path of the previous administrators, because they wanted to avoid being called “neo-dictators”. So, their problem was, they wanted to be dictators but wanted to be known by some honorable  name. At this state, they pleasure-fully discovered, in Socialism/Communism there is a post of  ‘party-chairman’, who by all means could act as dictator. It is no wonder that they immediately accepted Communism. Even though communism advocated for experienced organizers and party workers to held important posts, now ex-generals were in those posts. The administration, however, was quite efficient because it was formed with the experience of ‘military discipline’.          

 

CAPITALIZATION OF WEALTH TO BE OWNED BY FEW : CAPITALISM :

Adam Smith in his “Wealth of Nations” indicated the system of capitalization of wealth in industrial production. USA accepted this system first in industrial production. Later, it was applied in agriculture, as a result of which subsistence agriculture was replaced by ‘corporate farming’.

‘Capitalization’ is a general term that may indicate many things including the following :

DEMOCRACY : Capitalization of majority’s will.

SOCIALISM :     Capitalization of all men’s welfare.

COMMUNISM : Capitalization of working people’s absolute right.

CAPITALISM :  Capitalization of industrial and agricultural growth for optimum profit.

 

However, the word capitalization is mostly used in connection with Capitalism. Even though Capitalism is necessarily an economic system targeted for ‘profit optimization through industrial and agricultural growth’ there is a strongly-going wrong-conception that ‘Capitalism is a political system’. Some people even mention ‘Socialism’ and ‘Capitalism’ as counter parts. The fact however, is Socialism or Communism is an economic production system as well as political administration, where as Capitalism is only an economic production system, with no reference to political administration. In capitalist USA, the political administration is Democracy.

 

CAPITALIZATION AND DEMOCRACY :

In the past capitalistic countries often complained of lack of democracy in the Socialist countries. The ex-USSR was a communist country, where democracy had no scope to exist. The administration was a ‘single party administration’, and the Chairman, by all means was a dictator. How can it be democracy ?

 

When a single person had the absolute right to rule the country, it was possible for this ‘super-powerful’ man to do anything he liked. When provocation came from outside and when it was possible by the alien counties to ‘purchase’ this single man, the man shrewdly arranged the fall of the political system of the country.

 

While USSR deals with one political party, USA deals with two. The less the number of political parties in USA, the less the corporate bodies need to invest to manage them. After the election is over, USA also is governed by a single powerful person. USSR, by heart could feel and through total collapse could realize the problem, when a single ‘all powerful person’ stays at the top. In 2002, USA also felt what happens with such a system of administration. If there are traces of truth in “Fahrenheit 9/11” (Moore’s documentary) the possibility influencing the single person from outside cannot be ignored. Besides, the world witnessed, when the rogues were driving passenger planes to such important establishments as Pentagon, White House, Twin Tower etc. the appropriate authorities could not do anything. “Why ?” Because they could not make contact or collect clearance from the “all powerful man”. The later consequences have proved, in magnitude this failure was in no way less than the failure of ex-USSR caused by the single all-powerful man. There have been profuse changes in the ex-USSR after that failure. Now the country is proceeding to such a state that in future this country also would be in a position to endeavor purchase the ‘single powerful man at the top” in other countries.

       

ALL-POWERFUL MAN ON TOP, THE MOST DANGEROUS PROPOSITION  :

When the target is optimization of profit, in the honest way it can be done by using efficient and specialized workers and machineries, superior-quality seeds and fertilizers etc. In the dis-honest way the same can be done by avoiding all these cumbersome business and by purchasing the political leaders and bureaucrats of the rival and poor countries. Some countries are already after such ventures. After the changes of their political administration countries like China and Russia won’t have problem to proceed in the similar ways. In such venture, the “all powerful man on top”, irrespective of whether he is a product of democracy or a dictator, would be the target of purchase or destruction. Experiences show, the governments of the parliamentary system of democracy is comparatively safe, (because of their multiplicity of number, the alien government may find it difficult to purchase all), where as that of the presidential system is most vulnerable.       

 

COUNTRIES WITH BURNT HOUSES :

We started this article with the story of the meteors. Marx-Angels threw these meteors in the sky. Those successfully landed in some countries of Asia and Europe. The ‘generals turned to party chairman cum all powerful men on top’. With their experience in the armed forces they found it difficult to materialize what Marx-Angels conceived of Socialism. In such a situation the generals initiated a program of exporting revolution and in fact they did it in some countries. However, in most of these countries the revolution failed. Now, in such countries millions of mothers cry for their sons, who participated in the revolution and perished like sparking-meteors. The closed factories remind anyone the great economic loss of the country. And now, the very ‘exporters of revolutions’ have faded away. These are the countries where Meteor No. 02 descended. No wonder that the people mention it as “CURSE”. 

 

COUNTRIES WITH FRIED MEAT :

When Socialism/Communism was in practice in the east some intelligent people accepted its political ideology, wanted to use the same in the west and formed political parties. Alike the Communist party of the ex-USSR, in their party’s manifesto they declared profuse privileges for the poor people and the workers. Because of the existence of powerful countries like USSR and China the governments of the western counties could not ignore their activities. In order to ‘counter’ such parties the other political parties of the west had to declare and in fact give some privileges.

 

Capitalism is the system of capitalization of wealth, where the question of the welfare of men is not of much importance. Still, in order to counter the communist/socialist parties the ruling political parties of such countries had to attach importance on the common men’s welfare. And the common people of these countries still enjoy those benefits and privileges. The sparking meteors thrown from the east have roasted their meat, which they still relish. Do they ever feel, what made their profiteering-minded capitalistic-government to sanction those privileges for them ? Even Socialism does not know what it really did for the poor and weak people of some western countries. In the language of Rabindranath Tagore,

“It sparked, it flew and got lost,

That was its great delight”.
