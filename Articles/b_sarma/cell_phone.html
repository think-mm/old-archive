BEHIND THE RELIGIOUS INVENTION PACKAGE

Project : Cell Phone

(After “Islamic Cell Phone and the Next Islamic Inventions” written by Mr. Abul Kasem)

  Bijon B. Sarma

  

INTRODUCTION :

How does one encounter enemy ? One either surrenders saying “I was wrong, now I surrender to you”, or one does not say anything but silently takes its shelter. Both the gesture indicate, it has accepted defeat. Needless to mention such defeat means its peril.  Religions all over the world are known well for their constant and lifelong enmity with Science. Where Science stands on logic, observation, experiment and deduction, Religion stands on “Faith” and only faith. Naturally the two cannot co-exist. In this world Science has little place in religious countries. In the same way religious cannot survive in countries rich in Science. In such a context, if a religion for one reason or the other endeavors to attach its names with the creation of Science it indicates, the religion has agreed to the superiority of Science. Religious Cell Phone as mentioned by Mr. Abul Kasem indicates the religion’s inability to exist without the help of science. This is commendable. When the followers of such a religion would discover, their religion cannot do without Science they themselves would be attracted to Science. 

 

When Mr. Abul Kasem wrote an article titled “ISLAMIC CELL PHONE AND THE NEXT ISLAMIC INVENTIONS” he mentioned of some peculiar inventions, and people cannot but laugh to go through those. Laughing is good for health. But Mr. Abul Kasem does not know how much effort, how much money, how much intelligence the organizers had to invest behind such projects. After all inventions are the jobs of the infidels. In religion God is the only inventor, such that there can be no invention by human being.  Naturally, when a religious enterprise needs to use scientific inventions they need to take the help of the infidels. For obvious reasons they have to things in utmost confidence. That makes such projects more costly and complicated. And for reasons known to all, the complexities cannot be made public. Mr. Abul Kasem is an intelligent men. From only one religious invention he could imagine over fifty such inventions. In case he tries he can probably imagine the complexities also behind such projects. If he can imagine the right thing he would probably see the tears also behind the invention package. Here we shall mention two imaginary cases only. 

 

THE RELIGIOUS CELL PHONE PROJECT :

Religions fought against Science for a pretty long time. But when it realized, it cannot defeat science but science can defeat it some religions changed strategy and decided to take shelter in it. The birth of religious cell phone lies in this fact. Now one can see a successful religious cell phone in the hands of the followers. People become happy to see the satisfied faces of the users. But they do not know how much sweat, tears, energy and money were spent behind this project. This is the untold story behind. After the project was finalized and the oil rich Sheikh’s agreed to supply money, the believers had to look for the infidels, because the rush reality is, they are the real expert of the subject. They were given the religious sayings to be recorded in the cell phone. When they were about to manufacture the SIM card someone raised the most important question. He said, in religion prayer was the most important thing. Hence there must not be any communication during the prayer time. One raised the question : “What about important messages coming at this time ?” The reply from the horse’s mouth was : “For a religious person nothing can be more important than prayer”.

 

So, an instruction was sent to the SIM manufacturing company saying : THERE MUST NOT BE ANY COMMUNICATION DURING THE TIME OF PRAYERS. The instruction was associated with prayer times in relation to sunset and their durations. Within two days there came a query, “Previously you instructed, the cell phone would be applicable throughout the globe and not in any particular country. Do you still stick to it ?” The immediate reply that was sent was “Of course we do”. They were confident, universal religion believes in the universe and not in any particular area or country.

 

The SIM cards were secretly manufactured in an infidel country. After three months the box-full of cards reached the destination. Enthusiastic users made queue in the evening before the specified date. On the auspicious day the workers worked their best and all the SIM’s were sold before noon .  When they heard their religious hymns to come from a scientific gadget there appeared heavenly pleasure on the faces of the users. Oh what a divine scene it was.

 

The distributor thought of keeping their business closed on the following day. But they could not. The users who appeared came with complains and soon they formed a long queue. They reported, all the religious topics arranged in cell phone were working well. But it did not send or receive any call.

 

DIALOGUE :

It was a serious complain. The infidel manufacturer was immediately contacted. The dialogue that followed was the following :

RELIGIOUS SCIENTIST : What SIM cards have you supplied ? Why does not it send or receive calls?

INFIDFEL EXPERT : Look, we have tested and those are o.k.. Those however, are programmed not to receive or send any call during prayer time.  Do you remember you instructed like that ?

RELIGIOUS SCIENTIST  : Yes. But why does not it work even after prayer time ?

INFIDFEL EXPERT :  You are wrong. It definitely works when there is no prayer.

RELIGIOUS SCIENTIST : Look man, now it is ten in the morning. And I tell you this is not our prayer time. But still the damn thing it does not work.

INFIDFEL EXPERT : Who told you this is not prayer time ? This is the right time of prayer in Tanzania .

 RELIGIOUS SCIENTIST : So what ?

INFIDFEL EXPERT : Did not tell us to program it for global use ? Look, as per papers given to us your prayer times are fixed in times of Sun’s appearances in the sky. Various countries have their varying standard times. We have programmed the SIM card in relation with the standard times of all countries of the world. Now you check the prayer times of these countries and you would know the fact.

RELIGIOUS SCIENTIST : Oh my God. Look man, you always say complex and difficult things. Those are things I do not understand.

INFIDFEL EXPERT : Is that the reason why you have finally surrendered to us ?

RELIGIOUS SCIENTIST : Yes. But for God’s sake, sorry, I forgot you people do not believe in God. For Science’s sake please do not tell it to others. Now please explain the matter in easy, simple words.

INFIDFEL EXPERT : Look man, we programmed the SIM card in respect of prayer times in all the countries of the world. As you know, times in different countries vary because the hemisphere-shaped earth rotates on its axis tilted at 23.5 Deg. at a velocity of over 1000 miles per hour and completes its rotation around the Sun in 365 days 5 hours 57 minutes and 58 seconds, resulting in variation of day length in different countries, who in turn fix their standard times depending on the time observed on the particular latitude passing through their capital cities, such that the Sun is found to rise and set at various times resulting in various prayer times.

RELIGIOUS SCIENTIST : Please stop, stop. I cannot understand these complex things.

INFIDFEL EXPERT : That is obvious. Now I understand the reason. The account you presented to us has been prepared imagining an earth extending from one end of a desert to another end, where the Sun rises in the East and sets in the West. How can that simple religious account satisfy our complex scientific world ?

CONCLUSION :

“WITH MUCH TEARS, PAINS AND PETRODOLLARS ANOTHER SET OF SIM CARDS WERE MANUFACTURED. THIS TIME, BECAUSE OF OBVIOUS REASONS, THE RELIGIOUS SCIENTIST ALLOWED FULL FREEDOM TO THE INFIDEL EXPERT”. NEEDLESS TO MENTION, THE PROJECT WAS A GRAND SUCCESS.
