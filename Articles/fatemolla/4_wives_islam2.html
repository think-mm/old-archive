IRRESPECTIVE OF ANY ARGUMENT, ONLY THE QURA’ANIC NUMBER OF "UP TO 4" ALONE --CLEARLY, STRRONGLY AND CONCLUSIVELY PROVES THAT THE LAW WAS NOT MEANT TO HANDLE A CONTINUOUS SITUATION OF 1.08 TIMES. BECAUSE WOMEN WILL NEVER BE 4 TIMES OF MEN IN NUMBER.  

 

As men were not, are not, and never will be, even double than women in number, a number-based law of "up to 4" can not at all relate to reality. Considering the fact that in the Qura’an Allah allows Muslim men to have unlimited number of slave girls to sleep with, even a number of maximum 2 wives does not make sense.  Let us look at those very words of 4:3 now.  

"And if you fear that you can not act justly among the orphans- then marry those who seem good to you, two, or three, or four, and if you fear that you shall not deal justly (with so many) then (marry) one only….".  

Does it clearly say here that you are allowed to marry any woman of the whole world? No. Dose it clearly say you are not allowed?  No.  So, in this foggy situation let us look at three very important points. 

(1). The Hadis about the circumstance/time and the very purpose of the law. This Ayat was revealed after the "Ohud" war, where many Muslims were killed leaving behind many orphan girls. As recorded in Sahi Bokhari page-678-Hadis#2428:- When she was asked about the Ayat 4:3, Bibi Ayesha (RA) said :- "If the orphan girl is beautiful or rich, the caretaker himself marries her without giving suitable "Mohr" but they do not do that if she is not beautiful or rich. THIS AYAT IS REVEALED TO STOP THIS INJUSTICE (emphasis mine).  So, if "THIS INJUSTICE" of that situation to orphans does not exist, clearly the law  does not imply either.   

(2) Now, let me put forward some faint voices responding to humanity against this century old strong inhumanity towards our women. The clear use of  the words "orphan-girls" in 4:127  well explains  4:3.  " THEY ASK FOR YOUR PERMISSION TO MARRY WOMEN. TELL THEM, ALLAH PERMITS YOU ABOUT THEM AND WHAT IS NARRATED TO YOU, IS THE INSTRUCTION ABOUT THOSE FATHERLESS-GIRLS WHOM YOU WANT TO MARRY BUT DO NOT GIVE THE PRESCRIBED RIGHT… " ( from Bangla translation- Muhiuddin Khan, please check for "distortion!”). 

 

The explanation section of the Ayat 127 ( S.V. MIR AHMED ALI) says the same :-"This is directly related to 4:3 as in THE RIGHTS OF WOMEN REFERRED TO HERE ARE THOSE MENTIONED IN Vr.3………… Yataaman-Nisa MEANS THE GIRL-ORPHANS”.  

“””According to Maolana Omar Ahmad Osmani, verse 3 of the 4th chapter applies ONLY TO ORPHAN GIRLS AND WIDOWS AND DOES NOT ALLOW PLURALITY OF WIVES FROM AMONGST OTHER WOMEN. The argument is that, the definite article "al" in "alnissa" MUST REFER TO YATAMA i.e ORPHANS. OTHERWISE THE WORD "NISSA" WOULD HAVE BEEN SUFFICIENT TO CONVEY THE SENSE OF GENERALITY OF WOMEN.”””  …….. ….”The choice of wives for plural marriages MUST BE FROM AMONG THE WIDOWS OR ORPHAN GIRLS. IT IS ONLY IN THIS SENSE THAT THE RELEVANCE OF THE OPENING SENTENCE OF THIS VERSE IS JUSTIFIED. If this is read and considered with the preceding verse (4:2), the meanings become more clear. IT IS AGAINST THIS BACKGROUND THAT POLYGAMY HAS BEEN PERMITTED”. (Women’s Rights in Islam- Mohd. Sharif Chowdhury).  

" The permission to marry more than one wife is ….. in no way general in nature". --J. Aftab Hussain in " Status of Women in Islam",  

( 3) The preceding  sentence of  4:2   leave no way/room to use this law to marry other than orphan-girls. 4:2  deals only with the interest of the orphans. Then a sudden permission of marrying any woman of the whole world is not befitting. It could be befitting in Sura Nisa Ayats 19, 20,21,22, 23,  24, 25 and other places where the Qura’an tells us whom we can marry and whom we can not. We all know that many words of the Qura’an can be explained in more than one way. And unfortunately our rule-makers always took the ones against women.  

This is proven that if we get too much involved in dots of "i"s and cuts of "t"s of theology, we are sure to get lost.
