Zero Point

 Jahed Ahmed

 

What’s this difference?

Why’s this difference?

Let me ask you-

Don’t take offense.

Between you & me

Who created fence?

 

They created God

They say ‘Just and Fair,’

Commoners get murdered

God doesn’t care!

 

They created religion

They say is ‘for peace,’

Quran-Bible-Veda they hold

And they do kiss!

 

Such is the lust for God

And for Heaven,

They kill innocent kids

Men and women!

 

I reject this myth

I reject this tale,

I don’t like ‘God’s agents’

Nor do I hail!
