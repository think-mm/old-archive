A short project report of Motaharun maternal and Infant Care (MMIC)



Motaharun nesa Maternal & Infant care is a NGO which was registered with the Department of social services in June 20,2002.

Central Office of MMIC : 3/3, Block -A, Lalmatia, Dhaka-1217.

Project Office : Village -Belgachhi, Union- Sadar Union of Chuadanga, Upozila- Chuadanga Sadar, District- Chuadanga.


Description of the organization and project

Motarunnesa Maternal and Infant care (MMIC) was established in June2002 as an NGO g in sadar union of Chuadanga district. It is exclusively organized for providing health care services to the rural disadvantaged population. A large portion of population specifically the  impoverished, because of their economic condition and detrimental social norms are lagging behind to receive the Medicare services from the government health resource centers at the time of their need. Although enough provision of health services for the poor are kept in papers but because of powerful evil underhand dealings the poor are deprived of getting the benefit of health services and that takes many of them at the door step of death. Amongst these left behind patients in the rural areas the condition of the poor expected mothers and their new born child are most severe. The founder of MMIC is elderly man of village Belgachi of Chuadanga district. He has long been dreaming to mitigate the sufferings of these expected mothers and their new born child of this village and the surrounding. To fulfill his dreams he started constructing a 10 bed mother-child centre at this village Belgachi in the name of his deceased mother Motaharun Nesa. He duly shared his dreams with his relatives, nearest friends and energetic young people. They all appreciated their advice and renamed his mother-child care centre "Motaharun Nesa Mateenal and Infant Care' (MMIC) as NGO. The NGO was then duly registered with Department of Social services after completing all formalities of a NGO. It was discussed in a general meeting of MMIC.

That MMIC would work in tha field of (1) Primary Health Education (2)provide Medical facilities to the expected mother mothers and their new born child.




The prime objectives of the organization are as follows:

  • Educate the most vulnerable population of the target area with primary health care, hygiene and sanitation.
  • Identify the expected mothers who are affected by the detrimental social norms and became out reach from getting health benefit from government health resource center.
  • New born child to be given Medicare services up to the age of 12 months.


After registering with social services department MMIC was then started at Sadar Union of Chuadanga district.

One Field Coordinator and two Field Facilitators had been hired to start functioning the field activities. At the beginning, 10 Health Cadres were picked up from the field areas to get their voluntary services. The Health Cadres Assisted the Field facilitators (FF) in registering the names of beneficiaries who received primary Health Education. They also assisted the FF to register the expected mothers who would receive hospital services. of MMIC. The health Cadres (HC) had been given training on primary health education before working in the field to give awareness and motivation to the beneficiaries. The FF and HC then go to the field to provide awareness and motivation to the beneficiaries on primary health , hygiene and sanitation. Up till now 1000 beneficiaries have been given awareness and motivation on primary health , hygiene and sanitation. MMIC also organized monthly workshop/seminer traning for the beneficiaries on primary health , hygiene and sanitation.

Motaharun Nesa Maternal and Infant Care center have started giving the outdoor treatment to the expected mothers from beginning of last August. 1Part time doctor and 2 part-time nurses have been hired in this connection. 30 expected mothers have been registered as out door patients and being provided with outdoor treatment.
