Humayun Azad (28 April, 1947- 11 August, 2004): Some words of bereavement from Mukto-mona’s Advisory Board

Professor Humayun Azad, a distinguished member of Mukto-mona, had passed away on August 11, 2004, in Munich, Germany leaving us to grieve his death.  The professor was one of a rare breed in Bangladesh where freethinking and rationalism have taken a backseat.  Thanks to religious fervor and growth of extremism that has overshadowed whatever little freethinking was in the offing.  Professor Azad was a beacon of both hope and light in this dreadful time in a nation....
