The Hypocrisy of the Conservatives- Right Wing Political Group 

- Mohammad Arafat

 

Some Christian Fanatic called Jerry Falwell along with other Christian Fundamentalists of the same line claimed USA as a Christian nation. According to them it is the duty of the civil servants to preserve the dignity of their heritage as a Christian nation. Does the voice seem familiar? - The tone and the approach?  We hear all the time the similar tune from our radical Islamists in Bangladesh. In the convention where the voices of the fundamentalist Christian were uttered, were participated by innumerable so-called educated, polished, suited and booted people. By appearance, they seemed to represent the most sophisticated and educated class of America. Nevertheless, the speeches that conveyed the state of their mentality vividly reflected the blindness and the lack of knowledge of the other side of the coin. The other side of the coin can be referred as to the state and sufferings and the subtle segregation of the Muslim minority in the USA especially after 9/11. Evidently Falwell and gang always lambaste the people who are for separating the religion from the Sate i.e. secularism.

 

Their (fundamentalists) claims- USA is a Christian nation and those who are in effort to secularize the country are sinners. They believe the future civil servants of the country ought to understand the ‘holy bible’ to understand the basis of the constitution of the USA as well as the values on what USA is established. 

 

I had a conversation with a conservative Christian who happened to be a republican supporter and had a high regard for Mr. George Bush after the US election. He claimed that George Bush won the election on the ground of moral values. I asked him what did he mean by morality and who set the standard for morality. His answer was God. This is again the same blindness that grasps most of the human being into dark. I asked him what if others did not share his set of morality based on his belief how would he still call it morality. He was mum.

 

Not every body in the world believes in the same religion. Lots of people do not even believe in any religion at all. Yet it is a common trend among the most of the conservatives to impose their own set of believes on to others by legislating their own values into constitution. They want to do so by the power of their majority might.

 

Few days before some one in ‘Mukto-Alochona’ claimed ‘we Bangladeshis are majority Muslims. Isn’t it our democratic right to form a true Islamic country?’ He in fact wanted a referendum on the very issue and wanted peoples to decide whether Bangladesh should be a Muslim country and expected 86% Muslims of Bangladesh will vote for his agenda. Lots of radical Muslims in Bangladesh have the similar desire. Similar claims have been made by BJP (Hindu Radical Political Party) in India. Radical Christians in the USA also love to believe that USA is formed on Christian ideology and desire to create a true Christian country. All the groups mentioned above belong to a country where they are the majority in terms of religion and frequently ignore the rights of the minority. Being charged by the secularists of their disparaging attitude toward the minority, the fundamentalists sometimes feign ostensible and phoney sympathy to the minority. 

 

It is important to understand that democracy does not only mean vote or polls. The person who wanted referendum on whether or not Bangladesh should be a true Islamic country didn’t seem to understand the very notion of democracy. His demand about the referendum probably stems from his own position as a Muslim in Bangladesh and it’s overwhelmingly majority Muslim population. Democracy doesn’t mean poll as I said before, it is a state or condition where people enjoy equal rights. Separation of religion from Sate is a prerequisite for fruitful democracy. No matter how much security and safety is provided to the minority population in Bangladesh, imposing the Islamic rule would be an unfair treatment to them to make it an Islamic country in the first place. The minority populations, just because of being small in number would be pushed into live in a theocratic country where the country would be run by a believe system that they do not share. Such situation is a big discrimination to the religious minority population in the first place no matter how many pledges are made to provide security and safety. This idea is applicable for the minority Muslim population in India and in USA as well.

 

In the recent election in the USA republicans won the race because of playing the religious trick. Majority of Americans are conservative Christians, therefore, it was easy to win the race if the campaign was based on Bible. John Kerry being a liberal and a secularist chose to speak truth and fair as far as the religion or the religious issues were concerned. He said he is a Catholic but do not believe in transferring his article of faith on to other by making his faith into legislation. In my opinion, recognizing every body’s right is a big part of morality instead of any specific values dictated by any holy books, which is not shared by all. To me imposing Christian Faith in USA, Hindu Faith in India or Islamic Faith in Bangladesh and thereby ignoring the right of the minority population is oppression of the majority and the most immoral thing to do.

 

Howard Dean a progressive and secularist democrat called George Bush as the most right wing extremist in the recent history. George Bush proved to be a right wing extremist in several occasions. While asked in the presidential debate about what kind of person he wants to appoint as Supreme Court chief justice, he replied some one who understand the core value of the US constitution and do not misinterpret the constitution. What he meant was the interpretation of the constitution should be in light of Bible what he believed was the basis of their declaration of independence. We hear the similar kind of voices in Bangladesh but in our case Islam is implicated.

 

As democrats are known as liberals and secularist therefore their representative John Kerry replied to the same question by saying he wanted to appoint some one whose judgment would be so fair that the judgment would not reflect the judge’s own identity whether he is Christian, Muslim, Jewish or Black or White.

 

 

Right wing political parties are always for the majority population therefore the minorities do not feel secured under their rules. The example is in the USA the voice of right wings is – “ We are majority Christian let’s make it a Christian country or at least start the pledge of allegiance with God”. In Bangladesh the voice of right wings is – “We are majority Muslims lets make it a Muslim country, at least make Islam the sate religion or add Bismillah Hir Rahman ir Rahim in the constitution.  On the other hand the voice of Liberals tries to be fair to all. Therefore the liberals do not intend to exercise their majority might. According to the liberals and secularists all the religions should be protected and all the religious people should be treated equally in their state. The only way to do it is separating the religion from the state- Secularism.

 

It is worth noting about the abysmal hypocrisy of the right-wingers as well as their unfathomable double standard.  The voice of the liberals/leftists/secularists is universal. They support liberals everywhere they go. Right wing conservatives support the right wing political group in their own country where they belong to the majority. The same group of people is vigorously critical about the any other right wing political group of different religion of the other country. For instance, it was in the newspaper that the most of the Bangladeshi people living in the USA supported John Kerry in the US election. I have experienced and talked to so many people who bear right wing mentality but voted for Kerry when it comes to US election.  Does that mean these people suddenly became liberals and secular minded or leftists? Unfortunately that is not the case.  Something has changed but that’s not the mentality but the state of these people. The same people who belong to the majority class in Bangladesh became minority in the USA and minorities always feel unsecured under the rule of right wing political environment, therefore they turned for the liberals and leftists despite having right wing mentality. The gift of the liberals and the secularist is they are able to instill trust and security onto the minority by their fair way of thinking and neutrality.

 

There are some common provocative political practices that are exercised by the conservatives and right wing political parties. They often try to succumb their opponents by labeling them as the enemy of the religion. Such tactic is very effective especially if the majority of the population is very sensitive to religion and irrational. To give credence to this claim it is justified to mention the incident of George Bush and Dick Cheney’s false propaganda against John Kerry and Democrat. Bush and Cheney stated in some public gathering in the state of Arkansas that if Democrat comes to power Bible would be banned in the state. Syndicated columnist Mark Shield and House minority leader Nancy Pelosi substantiated in two different occasions about their opponents above assertions. 

 

If the politics is measured on a scale, which is often done, Liberals and Secularists always fall on the left side. Democrats are considered as leftists in the USA. Same formula applies in the case of Bangladesh. On the same scale BNP and four party alliances fall on the right side of the scale and are considered right-wingers. The purpose of this write-up was to expose the double standard of few who take different stand for their own benefit instead of taking the stand for right against wrong.
