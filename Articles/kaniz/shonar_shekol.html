As a young woman in Bangladesh, Mira dreamed of becoming a writer. But at sixteen she was married off to Habib, a budding businessman. He is nearly twenty years older than Mira. Her married life was full of trial and struggle until the death of Habib to cancer. Mira finds herself depressed, when Ajoy, a friend from her past, comes to visit her. Ajoy teaches in the United States and has come to Dhaka during a sabbatical leave.

As they fall in love, Mira revives her dream of being a writer. However, she is challenged by her society and conflicts arise. Complicated by love, social expectation, moral value and her aspiration, she has to look deep inside herself for answers.
