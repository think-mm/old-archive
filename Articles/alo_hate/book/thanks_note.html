A thank you note from Mukto-mona!

On behalf of Mukto-mona Publication we would like to thank the following people for all their efforts in publishing our first book in bangla 'Alo Hate Choliyache Adharer Jatri' (https://gold.mukto-mona.com/Articles/alo_hate/book/index.htm ):

 
  • Many thanks to Professor Ajoy Roy for editing the book and administering the whole publication process.  
  • Thanks to Mr. Mezbahuddin of Ankur Prakashani in Dhaka for Publishing the book for us .  
  • Thanks to reputed theoretical physicist of the subcontinent Professor Harun-ar Rashid for writing a preface of the book  
  • Thanks to distinguished artist Mr. Hashem Khan for agreeing to design the cover page of the book.  
  • Thanks to Bishwajit Saha for importing the book for New York Book festival on April 2nd and 3rd in such a short notice.  
  • Thanks to Alam Khorshed for  including the book in Bengalcommerce website, where one can purchase the book online.
  • And at the end we would like to thank the members of Mukto-Mona who provided support during mm_ publication's first publication effort.
  
MM publication Board

Saturday, April 01, 2006
