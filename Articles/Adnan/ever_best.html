The problem with selecting political person is as each person has many admirers, so is there are many people against them.

His political achievement should not be dismantled from his failures, but selecting a single person as *best ever* only shows us that we human beings actually love to see some super human and would like to worship them (means when we say somebody is best, then we do not find any faults in his/her deeds anymore. The admirers of that person start to believe that whatever He/She did was perfect). Not a single person from the list can be replaced with another to get the same yield as the original person had done.
