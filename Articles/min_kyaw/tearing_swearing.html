No More Tearing and Swearing

Min Kyaw
 

 

Every race on earth deserves to survive, I believe. As the Arab thrives, Israel should get a chance and so should the Palestinian. Every race should favor living in harmony. As the occupations of the Jew settlers must be criticized, the Arabs should give room for Israel to live in peace. Agreements should be made among nations and must be recognized by peoples. This is the universal law for peace, I believe. Agreement is not an easy outcome but it is possible if all sides commit and invest sufficiently.  

Without developing good relationship between non-Muslim and Muslim, both will not live in peace but tearing and swearing. As blaming others influencing against the Islamic movements, the Islamic leaders also need to recognize how their actions are a threat and it is causing other peoples’ concerns. Blaming doesn’t work to solve any problem. Only the recognition of the problems will lead to problem solving but this is only the first step. 

There are extremists on all sides who have gained a lot; this is not a good sign for peace. There are moderate leaders as well; they need massive supports; however, moderate people are not as active as the extremists so they are less visible- this is another bad sign unfortunately. I’m glad to see some progress in the Middle East but this is so fragile and threatened all the time. I wish the moderate people would get involved in the process by showing their concern. As the moderate are too far behind in activities and less active in showing their concern, it seems only the extremists exist on both sides- even journalism is not fair enough. I wish the Palestinians would achieve reasonable lives.  

As long as the Jews are showing their aggression toward other races and exploiting other nations, the whole world only has to see Israel as a fearsome nation; by this, Israel can achieve only hollow smiles and anti-Semitism but no criticism on those hostilities toward Jews will not ease the peoples’ minds. Israel should consider for both sides as long term strategy; or should blame nobody if only the negative effect of an-eye-for-an-eye is the best solution.  

If the US is only for the US’ interest, if the EU is for European’s interests, if the Arab world is for Islamic interests, if the Jews are only for Semitism and so on, who will solve the problems? I don’t blame the ordinary peoples who are usually busy in daily basic but the leaders who live on the fund of people, fed by people and elected by people should lead peoples to the right direction- peace- with no lie. Both ‘anarchical religion’ and ‘superficial politic’ are the real dangers of the world; usually both are led by demagogic leaders who use the people in various ways.  

And also the knowledgeable intellectuals should put their good wills and more efforts as they lead their audiences, readers and admirers. The patterns of leading-reflection-of-a-time indicate the level of joy of the publics. It is hard to crack the nuts of thickies as they take time to harden their shells in daily uselessness what always abundant in the surrounding. It’s usually costly for a good cause and a good change. But true good people even donated their lives. 

If moderate intellect is a rare species, moderate people is a rare species too. Then moderate writing won’t make a condition. As journalism is general reading of the public and even it is bias, who on earth will make enough moderate people from who read little or nothing about general politics and religions? If only the cheap intellects of the hollow headed leaders have the chance to guide the general publics, no need to wonder why they suffer too much. But the most extreme leaders with the best intellect in cunning will worsen the public behaviors. If winning is everything, peace will be nothing but to exist only in the ideology and dreams. If moderate peoples are endangered species, moderate writing will not make the significance in a period. As writing modest moderate themes, hopefully, moderate people will thrive and rule the world for true peace and harmony. I believe if the moderate leaders can insert reasonable moderate-mindset into the publics, gradually it will hold larger ground.
