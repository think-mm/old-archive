Mukto-Mona Copyrights Notice 

This is to notify all those concerned that lately, we have noticed that certain websites are publishing our writers' articles without ever showing the courtesy of seeking prior permission from Mukto Mona (MM) moderators and from the writers. Many writers have informed us that the articles in questions were written and sent exclusively for Mukto Mona, and not in all cases copy was sent to other websites. Unless a writer has sent a copy of original article himself or herself to a website other than Mukto Mona, publishing unauthorized version of any Mukto Mona article would be considered a gross infringement of the copyrights law and therefore, a disgraceful act.

Any one interested to republish/reproduce an original MM article is urged to write to the writer directly, and inform the MM moderator(s) in this regard. At the same time, writers are requested to notify Mukto-Mona of any/all unauthorized postings of their articles in other websites.

Your cooperation in this regard to preserve the intellectual copyright of an author is solicited.

Sincerely,

Moderators
