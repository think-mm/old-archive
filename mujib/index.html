Friends,

 

Today is 15th August, 2002, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman’s 27th death anniversary. We mourn the tragedy of the brutal assassination of Bangabandhu along with his family. It was one of the most gruesome killings of a leader in Bangladesh’s history.

 

One may not agree with his political ideology or some of his political decisions, but Bangabandhu’s innumerable contribution in our liberation struggle is undeniable. He should be remembered with love and respect for his extra ordinary charismatic leadership role in our independence struggle. We remember him, with much honour, as a great leader who led us to the path of freedom, gave assistance all of  us to taste the immeasurable joy of having sovereign Bangladesh.

 

 

We mourn the day, the tragedy of his brutal assassination!

 

Mukto-mona Moderators
