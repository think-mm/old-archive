Mad Meher Ali's Artwork
By Meher Ali
 

I'm not an artist by a long shot.  Whenever I get the blues, I get an insatiable urge to darw something.  Lately I have been thinking 'bout 9-11 and its aftereffect.  Life changed everywhere.  Many people are hurting financially.  Who's responsible for all this?  As I ponder, one name came strongly into my thoughts.  The world has become topsy-turvy because of one man's vision.  That person is none other than Osama bin Laden.

To "immortalize" him I sketched a portrait Osama and some more.  I hope you will like some of my artwork.  Without further ado, here is my portfolio on Osama bin Laden and others like him.
 
   
This man needs no introduction. He is the Guru of many born again Muslims allover the world

 

    
Here is a imaginary portrait of the person who started the whole thing 1400 years ago

 

  
While in Afghanistan Osama acquired many Bibis. He is seen here with a young one on the road to Kandahar

 

 
 
One of the followers of Osama is protecting the modesty of Bibi Hawas of Afghanistan. Don't they look 
cute in their Burqas?

  
The struggle and the lost road: This happened only after the Daisy Cutter bombs hit Osama's turf

  
Osama's tired soldiers hit the road for Tora Bora (circa December 2001)

 
 
America's deadly Daisy Cutter Bomb leveled many buildings in Afghanistan including some fine Mosques
 
   
The victims of 9-11 world over (drawn with a tinge of post-modernism viewpoint)

 

   
Murphy's Law shows up everywhere sooner or later. The sketch here is appropriately named: The Mule-Mula-Mullah"

 
 

 Well, these are the sketches I wanted to show to you through this web page.  I hope you liked some of these.  Osama bin Laden's name would go down in the history as a man who screwed up this world in the dawn of this millennium.  Well, I feel relieved now -- now that I've shown you my priceless artwork.  Have peace.  Salam, Shalom, auf Wiedersehen, Namaste, Bye now.

Meher Ali

November 11, 2002

Maryland, America
