Hi! My name is Rimon Dhar. I was born in Chittagong, Bangladesh in 1989. My bad luck is that I didn’t get the chance to live in Bangladesh for the rest of my life. I left Bangladesh for USA when I was only 8 years. Leaving so many friends and relatives behind was the hardest thing ever happened to me so far in my life. Now I am in the freshmen year in Fort Hamilton High School. In my school, I feel happy to see many other Bangladeshi students and love to think that we share same culture. But I am shocked about the fact that those Bangladeshi people don’t care much about our Bengali culture. They don’t care about Bangladesh. They are rather proud identifying themselves as an American than a Bangladeshi. It shows- they are not patriot. I am not satisfied with their attitudes. Once our ancestors fought for our motherland. They fought for the language Bangla. Anyway, I will do my job- to love and work for my country and its people.

 

 

        People say, Bangladesh has changed a lot over last few years. However, I would love to live with the memory of a Bangladesh which is the sweetest place to live in!
