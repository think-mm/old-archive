Rationalist Day  2005

 

Mukto Mona celebrates Rationalist Day  2005

Mukto-Mona, in solidarity with Science and Rationalists' Association of India is celebrating 1st March as a Rationalist Day for the second time. By its own virtue, the day is open to all to celebrate and reflect on the need of instilling ideals of rationalism, love and science in the minds of us, and our budding generations.  We feel honored to greet you, the rationalist, on this occasion.
