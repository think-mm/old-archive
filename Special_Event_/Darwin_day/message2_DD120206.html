Special Greetings for Darwin Day Celebration -2

Published on Darwin Day (February 12, 2006)

 

I congratulate Mukto-Mona for their spirit and effort to celebrate Darwin Day as an occasion to raise the global awareness of science & philosophy. One of the biggest problems mankind facing today is driven by the force of bigotry, blind obedience & fanaticism, and only knowledge of science and philosophy can show us the light. A scientific mind with an honest & truthful philosophical value can turn this world into a beautiful & exotic place to live. I appreciate Mukto-Mona’s views of science, reason & understanding very much in this context. 

Best wishes to all. 

Manju Biswas
Senior Scientist/Human Rights activist
Barr Pharmaceuticals, Inc.
New York
