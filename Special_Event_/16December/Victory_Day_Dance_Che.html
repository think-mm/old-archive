Do the Victory Dance 

Mondar M. M. A. NOVO  (Che)

Published on December 16, 2006

The shepherd dogs in green camouflage in fascist rage,
Are out again to put the Bengal tiger back in pious cage,
To make Bangla a land of senseless sheep,
Forever bleating and begging for their keep.

Bangalees, glorious 16th of December is here,
Who will rule the streets? -- come out, have no fear?
Look around you today -- this is the chance
Break the green cordon and do the victory dance!

Do not bleat but roar as epic freedom fighters,
The true Golden Bengal tigers that you are!
Remember 1971 and remember the fallen,
Rise and make history once again!

Don't let them make Bangla a land of sheep?
Don't leave things to the coin flip!
